Hello, I am Luyanda Jnr Zondi.

I am a full-stack web developer who started creating websites back in high school in 2019. Since then, I have developed more than 40 websites. Web development is more than just coding for me—it's an art. I take every project as a creative expression, blending logic and design to build something meaningful.

Beyond development, I am also a photographer. My passion for both technology and art drives me to see the beauty in every line of code, just as I do in every captured moment.

As an intermediate developer, I know that growth never stops. Every day, I discover something new in the world of development. I consider myself a fusion of an artist and a developer, a combination that brings me closer to mastering my craft.

Frequently Asked Questions

How did you make the background effect in this website?

I used Three.js to write WebGL faster, and the background effect is written in GLSL. If you are interested in GLSL, I recommend you read The Book of Shaders.

Where can I find your code?

I have a GitHub account, but my projects (including this website) are private repositories.

Can I use your code?

I don't allow anyone to use my code. I don’t want to say such a thing, but I got too many questions about that.

How did you learn web frontend and WebGL/GLSL?

By reading books and articles on the internet. Here is a list of web pages I recommend:

  • Three.js Examples
  • The Book of Shaders
  • Three.js Journey
  • Codrops

What libraries do you use?

GSAP for animations.

Three.js for WebGL.

Tweakpane for tuning parameters.

Alpine.js as my favorite JavaScript framework.
